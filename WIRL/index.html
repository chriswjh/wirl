About Us

 

Located at R1424, Technology Building, Web Information Retrieval Lab (WIRL), was founded by Dr. Jenq-Haur Wang in September 2007.

Recent research topics include

Web information retrieval, social Web mining, big data analytics, natural language processing, network and information security.

If you are interested in related topics, you are welcome to join our lab!
